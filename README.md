# Sales-Dashboard
Sales Analysis | Data Report – Executive Summary

The Sales Analysis Data Report provides a comprehensive and structured overview of sales performance across product categories, payment modes, geographic regions, and sub-categories. The report is designed to support strategic decision-making by delivering clear insights into revenue generation, profitability, and customer purchasing behavior.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The top-level KPIs present a high-level snapshot of overall business performance:

Total Cloth – 144K
Represents the total sales value generated from the clothing category.

Sum of Profit – 37K
Indicates the total net profit achieved across all sales transactions.

Sum of Amount – 438K
Reflects the cumulative sales revenue across all product categories.

Sum of Quantity – 5,615
Denotes the total number of units sold during the selected period.

These KPIs enable stakeholders to quickly assess financial performance and sales volume.

Category-wise Sales Distribution

The donut charts illustrate the proportional contribution of Electronics, Clothing, and Furniture to overall sales and quantity.

Purpose:

Identify high-performing categories

Compare revenue and volume distribution

Support category-level planning and resource allocation

Sales Amount by Payment Mode

This visual compares total sales amounts across various payment methods, including Cash on Delivery (COD), Credit Card, EMI, UPI, and Debit Card.

Key Insights:

Highlights customer payment preferences

Assists in evaluating the adoption of digital payment methods

Supports optimization of payment infrastructure and policies

Geographic Sales Analysis

The map visualization displays sales distribution across different regions in India, represented through proportional markers.

Purpose:

Identify high-revenue regions

Analyze regional demand patterns

Support location-based marketing and operational strategies

Sub-Category Performance Analysis

The sub-category section presents product-level performance across multiple segments such as Accessories, Phones, Saree, Shirts, Printers, Furnishings, and others.

Purpose:

Evaluate individual product performance

Identify top-selling and underperforming sub-categories

Support inventory management and product portfolio optimization

Business Impact and Use Case

This report enables organizations to:

Monitor sales and profitability trends effectively

Understand customer purchasing and payment behavior

Identify growth opportunities across regions and categories

Make informed, data-driven strategic decisions
